Family Welfare Center Renovation

Collected till now: ₹13,750/- of ₹45,000/- from 9 volunteers :)

Current Progress

I have completed the compound wall and the gate is placed. It costed me around ₹32,000/-. Hopefully, if possible I will try getting the paint job done and CCTV maybe? Not sure. Exhausted my budget for now :)

The Kutumba Kalyana Kendra located in the Horamavu Agara, Bengaluru, is a Family Welfare Centre by Government specialised in the free vaccination of kids up to 5 years of age. The centre is crucial for underprivileged people in the area and sees approximately 20 or more new mothers visiting with their babies twice every week on Tuesdays and Thursdays.

Problem

Due to lack of upkeep and neglect, the centre is in a poor state and in desperate need of betterment. The centre operates during the peak hot morning hours and there is no seating arrangement and the mothers have to stand in a queue in the hot sun while carrying their children. Besides this, due to the lack of proper walls and a gate, and due to the centre only being operational on two days of the week, the building has also started attracting unruly activities like people using it to drink, gamble, and even urinate. Such conditions are very detrimental to the physical and mental health of mothers and newborns.

Plan

To rectify these problems, I have decided to carry out a crowdsourced renovation project. The plan is to get the walls raised to 6ft and install a sturdy entrance gate, along with benches for the women and children to sit. These changes will help make the mothers and children comfortable and to keep the unwanted elements away from the premises.

Cost and Steps

The estimated cost of the project is ₹45,000.
