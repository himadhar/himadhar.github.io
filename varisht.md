<h3>1. Application Overview</h3>

<p><strong>Varisht</strong> is a modern, full-featured School Management System that provides a unified platform for managing all aspects of educational institutions—from student enrollment to financial operations, staff management, and administrative tasks.</p>

<p>It enables:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Student lifecycle management—profiles, enrollment, attendance, academic records, health records, fee tracking</li>
  <li>Academic operations—classes, timetables, lesson plans, assessments, library management</li>
  <li>Staff management—profiles, attendance, leave, payroll, scheduling</li>
  <li>Financial management—fee collection, payment gateway integration, scholarships, reporting</li>
  <li>Administration—transport, inventory, health & safety</li>
  <li>Analytics and reporting—dashboards, attendance analytics, financial reports</li>
  <li>Communication and events—messaging, announcements, parent-teacher meetings</li>
  <li>User and access management—role-based access, authentication, compliance, privacy settings</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Target users:</strong> School administrators, staff, students, and parents—suitable for K-12 schools, multi-campus systems, and educational organizations.</p>

<h3>2. Data Storage and Transmission</h3>

<h4>2.1 Cloud-Based Architecture</h4>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Server infrastructure:</strong> The application uses backend APIs and cloud deployment (AWS EC2).</li>
  <li><strong>Data transmission:</strong> User data is transmitted over HTTPS for authentication, CRUD operations, and real-time features.</li>
  <li><strong>REST APIs:</strong> Events, Finance Records, User Management, Compliance, Profile Calendar, Student/Staff data, Academics, and Analytics are accessed via REST APIs.</li>
  <li><strong>Real-time features:</strong> WebSocket (Socket.io) for live updates.</li>
</ul>

<h4>3.3 Payment Gateway</h4>

<p>Fee collection may integrate with third-party payment gateways. Payment processing is subject to the gateway provider's terms and privacy policy. Card or payment details are not stored by Varisht; they are handled by the payment provider.</p>

<h3>4. Access Control and Security</h3>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Role-based access:</strong> Administrators, staff, students, and parents have access scoped to their role.</li>
  <li><strong>Authentication:</strong> Secure login and session management.</li>
  <li><strong>Compliance and privacy settings:</strong> Built-in compliance management and configurable privacy controls.</li>
</ul>

<h3>5. Third-Party Services</h3>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Payment gateway:</strong> Used for fee collection; payment data is processed by the provider.</li>
  <li><strong>Cloud hosting (AWS):</strong> Application and data may be hosted on AWS EC2; environment-based configuration applies.</li>
</ul>

<h3>6. Internationalization</h3>

<p>Multi-language localization (i18n) is supported. Language preference may be stored as part of user or institutional settings.</p>

<h3>7. Summary</h3>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Cloud-based system:</strong> Data is stored and processed on servers; access requires authentication.</li>
  <li><strong>Role-based access:</strong> Users see only data appropriate to their role.</li>
  <li><strong>Payment processing:</strong> Fee payments use third-party gateways; payment details are not stored by Varisht.</li>
  <li><strong>Data retention:</strong> Governed by institutional policy and applicable regulations.</li>
  <li><strong>No sale of data:</strong> User and institutional data are not sold to third parties.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Overview</h2>

<p><strong>Varisht</strong> is a modern platform for managing all aspects of educational institutions, from student enrollment to financial operations.</p>

<h3>Mission</h3>

<p>To empower educational institutions with comprehensive data control and actionable insights—enabling administrators to make informed decisions, students to receive holistic academic feedback, and parents to participate in their child's educational journey through transparent, data-driven awareness.</p>

<h3>Vision</h3>

<p>To become the leading School Management System that transforms educational administration through superior data governance, gives students complete visibility into their academic growth, and fosters collaborative parent engagement through SWOT analysis and real-time insights.</p>

<h3>Objectives</h3>

<h4>For School Administration</h4>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Superior Data Control:</strong> Granular control over institutional data—student records, financial operations, staff management, and academic performance metrics</li>
  <li><strong>Comprehensive Analytics:</strong> Data-driven decisions via customizable dashboards, advanced reporting, and predictive analytics</li>
  <li><strong>Centralized Management:</strong> One platform for student lifecycle, academics, staff, finance, transport, inventory, and health & safety</li>
</ul>

<h4>For Students</h4>

<ul>
  <li><strong>360-Degree Academic Feedback:</strong> Holistic insights from assessments, attendance patterns, subject-wise analytics, and progress tracking</li>
  <li><strong>Self-Awareness & Growth:</strong> Understand strengths, identify improvement areas, and track academic journey via performance dashboards</li>
  <li><strong>Transparent Communication:</strong> Seamless communication with teachers and real-time access to resources, schedules, and feedback</li>
</ul>

<h4>For Parents</h4>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Comprehensive SWOT Awareness:</strong> Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats analysis of their child's academic performance, attendance, and behavior</li>
  <li><strong>Proactive Engagement:</strong> Real-time updates on academic progress, attendance, fee status, health records, and school events</li>
  <li><strong>Collaborative Partnership:</strong> Stronger parent-school collaboration through transparent communication and shared academic insights</li>
</ul>

<h2>Core Capabilities</h2>

<p>Everything you need to manage your educational institution efficiently.</p>

<h3>1. Student Management</h3>

<p>Complete lifecycle management: profiles, enrollment, attendance tracking, academic records (grades, transcripts, assessments), health records, document management, and fee tracking. Daily attendance, attendance reports, and subject-wise performance analytics.</p>

<h3>2. Academic Operations</h3>

<p>Classes and courses, timetable and schedule management, lesson plans, materials and syllabus distribution, exam schedules and assessments. Full library management—catalog, issuing and returning, overdue tracking, digital resources.</p>

<h3>3. Staff Management</h3>

<p>Staff profiles, qualifications, attendance and leave management, payroll processing (salary details, payslips, deductions, bonuses), scheduling and timetables, document management.</p>

<h3>4. Financial Management</h3>

<p>Fee collection and tracking with payment gateway integration, scholarship and grant management, financial reporting (monthly and annual, income and expense). Comprehensive finance record APIs.</p>

<h3>5. Administration</h3>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Transport:</strong> Route and schedule management, student bus allocation, GPS tracking, boarding and de-boarding attendance</li>
  <li><strong>Inventory:</strong> Asset tracking, resource allocation, maintenance requests, procurement and vendor management</li>
  <li><strong>Health & Safety:</strong> Medical records, immunization tracking, incident reporting, emergency contacts, health checkups</li>
</ul>

<h3>6. Analytics & Reporting</h3>

<p>Performance dashboards for students, staff, and external partners. Attendance analytics, financial reports, customizable dashboards with data visualization.</p>

<h3>7. Communication & Events</h3>

<p>Event management with calendar integration, messaging (direct messages, group chats), announcements (school-wide and class-specific), parent-teacher meeting scheduling.</p>

<h3>8. User & Access Management</h3>

<p>Role-based access control (RBAC), user management, authentication and authorization, compliance management, privacy settings. Multi-language localization (i18n) supported.</p>

<h3>9. Profile Management</h3>

<p>Personal profile management, document storage, integrated calendar with event management.</p>
